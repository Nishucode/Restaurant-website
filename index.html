Burritos

Flour tortillas filled with your choice of protein (chicken, beef, or pork) and Spanish rice. A fan favorite!

Enchiladas

Our authentic Mexican enchiladas are made with corn tortillas filled with your choice of savory shredded chicken or beef, and smothered in flavorful red or green sauce. Topped with shredded cheese and served with rice and beans. A delicious and filling meal!

Fajitas

Sizzling hot and bursting with flavor, our fajitas feature tender strips of marinated chicken or beef, grilled with onions and bell peppers. Served with warm tortillas, lettuce, shredded cheese, sour cream, and guacamole. Build your own perfect bite with all the fixings!
